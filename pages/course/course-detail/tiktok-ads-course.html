How is TikTok Ads different from Facebook Ads?

TikTok requires a completely different creative approach focused on authentic, entertaining content rather than direct sales messaging. The platform also has younger demographics and unique ad formats we'll cover in depth.

What budget do I need to start?

You can start testing with as little as $20/day. We recommend $50-100/day for proper testing. The course includes budget strategies for all levels.

Do I need to be good at video editing?

Not at all! We teach simple creation tools and provide templates. Many top-performing TikTok ads are raw, unpolished videos shot on phones.

Is this course suitable for service businesses?

Absolutely! While e-commerce sees great success, we've included specific modules for local services, apps, and B2B lead generation via TikTok Ads.
