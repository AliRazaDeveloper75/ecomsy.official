Do I need any design experience?

No prior experience is required. We start from the absolute basics and progressively build your skills through hands-on projects.

What software do I need?

You'll need Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign. We provide guidance on getting student discounts for Creative Cloud.

What computer specs are recommended?

A computer with at least 8GB RAM (16GB preferred), dedicated graphics card, and SSD storage will provide the best experience for design work.

Will this help me get design jobs?

Yes! The course includes portfolio development, resume tips, and freelance business guidance to help you start getting paid work.
