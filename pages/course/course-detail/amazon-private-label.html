How much capital do I need to start?

We recommend starting with $3,000-$5,000 for your first product, which covers inventory, branding, and initial marketing. Some students start with less by choosing smaller products.

Do I need to hold inventory myself?

No, Amazon FBA handles storage and shipping. You'll send your products to Amazon's warehouses, and they'll handle the rest. We'll teach you how to manage inventory levels effectively.

How long until I see profits?

Most students see their first sales within 2-4 weeks after launch, with profitability typically achieved within 3-6 months depending on your product and marketing strategy.

What if my product doesn't sell?

We teach proven product research methods to minimize this risk. If a product underperforms, we'll show you how to reposition it or liquidate inventory strategically.
