How soon will I see results?

Search campaigns can show results within 24-48 hours. Display and YouTube campaigns typically take 7-14 days to optimize. We provide benchmarks for different industries.

What's the minimum budget to start?

You can start testing with $10-20/day for Search. We recommend $30-50/day for proper testing across campaigns. Local service businesses can start with smaller budgets.

Do I need design skills for Display ads?

No - we provide templates and show how to create effective ads using free tools. Google's responsive ads also automatically adapt your assets.

Yes! The course includes complete training on Performance Max campaigns, including asset optimization, audience signals, and performance analysis.
