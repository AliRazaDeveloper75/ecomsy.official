HOW IT WORKS?

Code Huddle guides you every step of the way,
from the inception of your idea to its completion. Here's a breakdown of it...

01

Ideation

You have the idea, and we’re more than ready to listen to it. In our first meeting, we discuss your innovative idea and everything that goes with it.

02

Requirement Analysis

Our requirement gathering specialists sit with you and gather all the essential requirements that you expect from your future product.

03

Proof-Of-Concept

Code Huddle strives to develop a POC to show our esteemed clients the achievability factor of their idea, making things infinitely clear and financially secure for them.

04

Development

Once all the requirements are validated, our experts start the designing of software architecture and ultimately the development of your product.

05

Testing

In addition to unit testing in the development phase, Code Huddle starts full-cycle testing to ensure optimal usage and quality of your solution.

06

Production

Our association with clients doesn’t just end with the completion of their projects. We keep a complete check on the solutions we make and assure their productivity after their deployment as well!
